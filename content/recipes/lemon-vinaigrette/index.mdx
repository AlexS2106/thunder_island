<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 tbsp lemon juice
  - ¼ tsp salt
  - ⅛ tsp black pepper
  - ⅛ tsp dried mustard powder

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Mix all the ingredients.
  - **Step 2**
    - Pour over the salad and toss.
  - **Step 3**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
