### Ingredients

- 100 g butter
- 1 x clove garlic
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- Â½ tsp crushed chillies

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Crush the garlic.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Heat a tbsp of the butter in a small frying pan on a low heat. (Number 3.)
  - Gently cook the garlic, until slightly brown.
  - Turn the heat down. (Number 2.)
  - Add the remaining ingredients, including the rest of the butter.
  - Stir until the butter is melted. Note that it won't completely mix together.
  - Serve in a shallow dipping bowl.

</Instructions>
