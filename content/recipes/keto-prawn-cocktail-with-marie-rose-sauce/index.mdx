<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 400 g prawns
- 4 x (large) iceberg lettuce leaves
- 1 x tomato
- 1 x avocado
- 20 ml extra light olive oil
- ½ egg
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- ½ tsp white vinegar
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1 tbsp tomato puree
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- **Optional**
  - pinch cayenne powder

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Wash and peel the prawns.
- Shred the lettuce.
- Slice the tomato.
- Slice the avocado.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - For the marie rose sauce, place the olive oil and egg into a bowl.
  - Using a handheld blender, whisk until the two are mixed and creamy.
  - Add the Dijon mustard, vinegar, salt, tomato puree and lemon juice and briefly blend again.
- **Step 2**
  - Mix the prawns and the marie rose sauce.
- **Step 3**
  - Divide the lettuce and place it at the bottom of four bowls.
  - Spoon the prawn mix over the lettuce leaves.
  - Arrange the avocado and tomato slices over the top of the prawns.
- **Step 4**
  - Serve chilled with the cayenne pepper sprinkled on top, or omit it if you prefer.

</Instructions>
