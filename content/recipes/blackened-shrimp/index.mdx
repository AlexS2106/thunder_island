<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 400 g shrimp
- ½ tbsp lard
- 2 tsp smoked paprika
- ½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp dried chives
- ¼ tsp dried basil
- ¼ tsp dried thyme
- ¼ tsp black pepper
- ¼ tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp cayenne pepper (use more/less or omit, as desired)

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Mix the blackening spices, smoked paprika, garlic, salt, chives, basil, thyme, black pepper and cayenne pepper in a bowl.
  - Mix the shrimp into the blackening spices, and cover each shrimp completely.
- **Step 2**
  - Melt the lard on medium to high heat. (Number 8.)
- **Step 3**
  - When the lard is hot, fry the shrimp for about 2 mins on each side until cooked.
  - They should turn pinkish when done.
- **Step 4**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
