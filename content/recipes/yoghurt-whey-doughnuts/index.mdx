<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- **For the doughnuts**
  - 150 ml yoghurt whey
  - 150 g self raising flour
  - oil
- **For the glaze**
  - 2 tbsp icing sugar
  - 2 tsp milk
  - Â½ tsp vanilla essence

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Make some yoghurt.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Place the oil in a small pan.
  - Heat on the hob on a medium heat. (Number 6.)
- **Step 2**
  - Add the yoghurt whey and self-raising flour together in a bowl and use two dessert spoons to mix them together.
  - It will look like a gooey mess.
- **Step 3**
  - When the oil is hot, use one spoon to scoop up some of hte mixture and use the other to push the mixture off the spoon into the oil.
  - Doughnut shaping is not a consideration here.
- **Step 4**
  - After 1-2 mins the doughnut should be done on one side. Turn it over carefully, use a fork.
  - Cook the other side for 1-2 mins.
  - Be careful not to overcook them.
  - Continue until all the doughnuts are done.
- **Step 5**
  - Set the doughnuts aside to cool, and meanwhile, make the glaze.
  - Note that the glaze is a necessary addition for taste.
- **Step 6**
  - Mix the icing sugar, vanilla essence and milk together to form a runny paste.
  - Randomly spoon the glaze over all the doughnuts, make sure not to miss one
- **Step 7**
  - Serve immediately these do not keep well.

</Instructions>
