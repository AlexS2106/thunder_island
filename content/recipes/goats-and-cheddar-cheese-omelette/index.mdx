### Ingredients

- 3 x eggs

- 50 g goat's cheese
- 25 g cheddar cheese
- 1 tsp butter

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Grate the cheddar cheese.
- Grate the goat's cheese.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Melt the butter in a medium frying pan over medium heat. (Number 6.)
- **Step 2**
  - In a small bowl, mix the eggs and salt.
- **Step 3**
  - When the butter is hot, tip the pan, so the butter covers the bottom.
  - Pour the eggs into the pan and tip again, so the mixture spreads.
  - Cook until the top of the eggs is just set, about 5 mins.
- **Step 4**
  - Use a fish slice and turn the omelette over.
  - Add the cheddar cheese and goat's cheese to one side of the omelette.
  - Leave the omelette to cook for 2 minutes.
- **Step 5**
  - Use a fish slice and fold the omelette in half.
  - Leave to cook for 1 minute.
- **Step 6**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
