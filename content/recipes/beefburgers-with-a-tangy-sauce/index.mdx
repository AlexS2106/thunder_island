<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- **For the burger**
  - 500 g beef mince
  - 100 g (½ large) yellow onion
  - 1 x egg
  - 25 g flour
  - ½ tsp salt
  - ½ tsp black pepper
  - ½ tsp mustard powder
  - ½ tbsp lard
- **For the tangy sauce**
  - 5 tbsp mayonnaise
  - 1 tbsp tomato puree
  - 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Finely dice the onion.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Mix all the ingredients for the tangy burger sauce and place the sauce in the fridge.
- **Step 2**
  - In a large bowl, mix the beef mince, onion, egg, flour, salt, pepper and mustard powder.
  - Squish the burger mixture through your fingers until the texture becomes smooth and soft.
  - Make four large burgers about 2cm (¾ inch) thick from the mixture.
- **Step 3**
  - Melt the lard in a large frying pan on medium-high heat. (Number 7.)
  - Using a fish slice, slide all the burgers into the pan and fry for 3½ minutes.
  - Carefully turn the burger over and fry for another 3½ minutes.
- **Step 4**
  - Serve the beefburgers with the tangy burger sauce on top.

</Instructions>
