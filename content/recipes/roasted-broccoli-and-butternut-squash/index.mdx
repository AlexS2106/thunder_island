<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 450 g (½ medium) butternut squash
- 60 g (1 medium) tomato
- 100 g (½ medium) broccoli
- 40 g (3 medium) spring onions
- 1 tsp curry powder (any strength)
- ½ tsp salt
- 2 tbsp oil

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Peel, de-seed and dice the butternut squash.
- Chop the broccoli into small florets.
- Dice the tomato.
- Thickly slice the spring onions

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 200°C.
- **Step 2**
  - Mix all ingredients and place in a large casserole dish.
- **Step 3**
  - Roast the vegetables for 40 mins, and stir part of the way through.
- **Step 4**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
