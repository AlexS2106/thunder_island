<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 2 x chicken breasts
  - 1 tbsp flour
  - ½ tsp salt
  - ¼ tsp black pepper
  - 3 x bell peppers (green, red, yellow)
  - 3 x tomatoes
  - 1 x onion
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 2 tbsp tomato puree
  - 2 x cloves garlic
  - 100 ml single cream
  - 240 ml chicken stock
  - 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  - ½ tsp salt
  - ½ tsp black pepper
  - 1 tsp sweet paprika
  - 1 tsp dried thyme

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Dice the chicken breast.
  - Dice the onion.
  - Slice the bell peppers.
  - Roughly chop the tomatoes.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Heat the olive oil in a large frying pan.
  - **Step 2**
    - Season the chicken breast with ½ tsp salt and ¼ tsp black pepper and cover with flour.
    - Cook in the pan until juices run clear, about 5 mins.
    - Remove the chicken, cover and set aside.
  - **Step 3**
    - In the same pan, cook the peppers and onion will soft, about 5-10 mins.
  - **Step 4**
    - When the onions and peppers are soft, add the tomatoes, and cook till soft.
  - **Step 5**
    - Add all the remaining ingredients, including the cooked chicken and simmer for 45 minutes.
  - **Step 6**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
