<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 1 x butter lettuce
  - 3 x oranges
  - 12 x cherry tomatoes
  - 1 x yellow bell pepper
  - 2 x avocados
  - 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  - 1 tsp dried basil
  - ½ tsp salt
  - ½ tsp black pepper

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Roughly chop the butter lettuce.
  - Peel the oranges and cut the segments in half.
  - Cut the tomatoes into quarters.
  - Dice the yellow pepper.
  - Dice the avocados.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Add all the vegetables and fruit to a large salad bowl.
  - **Step 2**
    - Mix the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, black pepper and basil separately.
  - **Step 3**
    - Add the balsamic vinaigrette to the salad and mix.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
