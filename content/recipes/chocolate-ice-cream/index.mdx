### Ingredients

- 500 ml cream
- 350 ml milk
- 200 g sugar
- 50 g cocoa powder
- 1 tsp vanilla essence

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Whisk the cream until stiff.
- **Step 2**
  - In a separate bowl, whisk the milk, sugar, cocoa powder, vanilla essence and salt until the sugar and cocoa powder are mixed in.
- **Step 3**
  - Fold the egg whites into the cocoa mixture until fully incorporated.
- **Step 4**
  - Chill in the fridge for 2 hours.
- **Step 5**
  - Make according to the ice cream machine instructions.
  - Our icecream machine is too small and needs two churnings for this.
- **Step 6**
  - Serve as a sloppy or place in the freezer for a couple of hours.

</Instructions>
