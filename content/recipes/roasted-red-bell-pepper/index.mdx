<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 1 x red bell pepper
- ¼ tsp oil

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Pre-heat a fan-assisted oven to 200°C.
- **Step 2**
  - Cut the red bell pepper in half, rub both halves with the oil and place both sides face-down on a baking tray.
- **Step 3**
  - Roast for 25 mins.
- **Step 4**
  - Remove the stalk and pips before serving.

</Instructions>
