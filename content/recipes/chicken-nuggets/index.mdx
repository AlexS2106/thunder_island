<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 450 g minced chicken
- 1 x egg
- 30 g panko breadcrumbs
- 4 tbsp vegetable oil

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Heat the vegetable oil in a medium-sized pan over medium-high heat. (Number 7.)
- **Step 2**
  - Mix the chicken mince and egg.
- **Step 3**
  - Place the breadcrumbs onto a plate.
- **Step 4**
  - Take about 1 tbsp of the chicken-egg mince and roll it into a ball.
  - Roll the ball in the breadcrumbs and set aside.
  - Repeat until all the chicken mince is in breadcrumb balls.
- **Step 5**
  - Place the chicken nuggets into the oil and fry for 2 mins on one side.
  - Flatten the chicken balls to about 1 cm thickness and fry for another minute.
  - Gently turn the nuggets over and fry for 3 mins on the other side.
- **Step 6**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
