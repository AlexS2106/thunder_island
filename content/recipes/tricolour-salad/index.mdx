<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - ½ medium iceberg lettuce
  - 1 medium avocado
  - 1 medium mango
  - 1 medium red bell pepper
  - 20 g red currants
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 2 tsp white vinegar
  - ⅛ tsp salt
  - ⅛ tsp black pepper

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Peel, de-seed and dice the avocado, mango and red bell pepper.
  - Shred the lettuce.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Take a small piece of the mango and crush it to make approximately 2 tsp of mango juice, and add the liquid to a small bowl. 
    - Add the olive oil, white vinegar, salt, and pepper to the bowl.
    - Mix everything thoroughly.
  - **Step 2**
    - Add all the ingredients to a large salad bowl and mix thoroughly.
  - **Step 3**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
