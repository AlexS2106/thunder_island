<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 250 g flour
- 200 ml warm water
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp active yeast

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- If you have a non-stick loaf tray, skip this step; if you don't, grease a baking sheet and line the tray with the sheet.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Prepare a bread tray of about 23cm in length.
- **Step 2**
  - Mix the flour, salt and yeast in a bowl.
  - Add the water and mix in.
- **Step 3**
  - Leave in a warm place to rise for 1-2 hours .
- **Step 4**
  - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 180Â°C.
- **Step 5**
  - Bake the bread for 45 mins.
- **Step 6**
  - Let it cool a little before slicing.

</Instructions>
