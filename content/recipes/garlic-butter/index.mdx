### Ingredients

- 100 g butter
- 1 x clove garlic

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Crush the garlic.
- Leave the butter out of the fridge to soften.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Mix the butter and garlic together.
  - Use as needed.
