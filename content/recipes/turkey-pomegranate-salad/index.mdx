<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- **For the turkey**
  - 300 g turkey breast
  - ½ tbsp lard
  - ½ tsp dried sage
  - ½ tsp dried parsley
  - ½ tsp dried thyme
  - ½ tsp dried rosemary
- **For the salad**
  - ¼ head butter lettuce
  - 1 x red bell pepper
  - 1 x tomato
  - 1 x orange
  - 1 tbsp pomegranate seeds
- **For the vinaigrette**
  - 50 g pomegranate seeds
  - 30 ml olive oil
  - 1 tbsp honey
  - ½ tsp apple cider vinegar
  - ½ tsp Dijon mustard
  - pinch salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Remove the turkey from the fridge 30 mins before cooking.
- Tear up the lettuce.
- Dice the bell pepper.
- Dice the tomato.
- Peel and slice the orange.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Heat the lard in a medium frying pan over medium-high heat. (Number 8.)
- **Step 2**
  - Mix the sage, parsley, thyme, and rosemary; rub the herbs over the turkey breast and add the meat to the frying pan.
  - Fry for 10 mins; turn over halfway through.
  - Remove from the heat when the juices run clear.
- **Step 3**
  - In a small bowl, mix the vinaigrette ingredients.
- **Step 4**
  - Mix all the salad ingredients except the pomegranate seeds in a medium-sized bowl.
  - Add the vinaigrette to the salad and toss.
- **Step 5**
  - To serve:
    - Slice the turkey breast.
    - Divide the salad between two plates and place half the breast on each.
    - Scatter the pomegranate seeds over the top.

</Instructions>
