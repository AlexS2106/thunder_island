<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 1.2 kg boneless pork loin
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 tsp salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Remove the meat from the fridge 30 mins before cooking.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 200°C.
- **Step 2**
  - Lightly score the meat with a sharp knife; stab the thicker bits of fat instead.
  - Rub the salt into the meat on all sides.
  - Rub the meat again with the oil.
- **Step 3**
  - Place the tray in the oven.
  - Cook for 50 mins until a meat thermometer reads 70° C or above.
  - Wrap the cooked meat in aluminium foil and rest for 30 mins.
- **Step 4**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
