<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 500 g lamb mince
  - 60 g (¼ medium) fennel 
  - ½ tbsp dried parsley
  - ½ tsp salt
  - ½ tsp black pepper
  - ⅛ tsp ground cinnamon
  - ⅛ tsp allspice

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Finely shred the fennel. (I use the food processor.)

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 200°C.
  - **Step 2**
    - Precook the fennel for 1 min in the microwave.
  - **Step 3**
    - In a large bowl, mix all the ingredients.
    - Squeeze the ingredients in your hands until the texture becomes smooth.
    - Shape the mixture into 15-20 small meatballs.
  - **Step 4**
    - Spread the meatballs out on a baking tray.
    - Roast in the oven for 25 mins turning halfway through.
  - **Step 5**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
