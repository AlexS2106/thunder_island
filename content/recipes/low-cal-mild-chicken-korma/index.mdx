<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 250 g chicken breast
- 100 g onion
- 100 g green beans
- 100 g cauliflower
- 100 g carrots
- 60 ml passata
- 180 ml water
- 1 tbsp lard
- ½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp black pepper
- 2 x cloves garlic
- ½ tsp ground ginger
- ½ tsp garam masala
- ½ tsp ground coriander
- ½ tsp ground turmeric
- 3 tbsp natural yoghurt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Dice the chicken breast.
- Dice the onion.
- Chop the cauliflower.
- Slice the green beans into 1 cm long pieces.
- Slice the carrots.
- Crush the garlic.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Set a saucepan to boil.
  - Parboil the carrots, cauliflower and green beans for 15 mins.
- **Step 2**
  - Melt the lard in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. (Number 7.)
- **Step 3**
  - Season the diced chicken with the salt and pepper.
  - Fry the chicken until done, about 10 mins.
  - Remove the meat from the pan, cover and set aside.
- **Step 4**
  - Add the onions and fry till soft, about 5 mins.
- **Step 5**
  - Add the garlic, ginger, garam masala, coriander and turmeric.
  - Fry for 1 min.
- **Step 6**
  - Add the passata and water.
  - When the sauce starts to simmer, re-add the chicken.
- **Step 7**
  - Turn the heat to medium-low and cover.
  - Leave to simmer for 10 mins.
- **Step 8**
  - Remove the cover, add the yoghurt and leave to simmer for another 5 mins.
- **Step 9**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
