<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- **For the salad**
  - 1 x iceberg lettuce
  - 1 x red bell pepper
  - 1 x avocado
  - 3 tbsp bilberries
- **For the vinaigrette**
  - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 tbsp lemon juice
  - ¼ tsp salt
  - ⅛ tsp black pepper
  - ⅛ tsp dried mustard powder

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Finely shred the lettuce.
- Dice the red bell pepper and avocado.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - In a large bowl, mix all the salad ingredients.
- **Step 2**
  - In a smaller bowl, mix all the vinaigrette ingredients.
- **Step 3**
  - Add the vinaigrette to the salad and mix.
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
