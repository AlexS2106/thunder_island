<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 500 g beef mince
- 110 g (1 x medium) onion
- 4 x cloves garlic
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 400 g (1 can) chopped tinned tomatoes
- ½ cup milk
- ½ tsp lard
- ½ tsp smoked paprika
- ½ tsp chilli flakes
- pinch salt
- pinch black pepper
- 1 x bay leaf

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Dice the onion.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Heat the lard in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. (Number 7.)
- **Step 2**
  - Fry the onion until soft, about 5 minutes.
- **Step 3**
  - Add the beef mince, fry for 5-10 mins until the meat is brown.
- **Step 4**
  - Add the tinned tomatoes, tomato paste, garlic, bay leaf, paprika, chilli flakes, salt and pepper.
  - Leave to simmer for 15 mins.
- **Step 5**
  - Add the milk.
  - Heat.
- **Step 6**
  - Remove the bay leaf.
- **Step 7**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
