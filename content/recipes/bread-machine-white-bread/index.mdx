<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 500 g plain white flour
- 300 ml warm water
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp veg oil
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 Â½ tsp active yeast

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Add the warm water to the bread machine first.
  - Add the honey and veg oil second.
  - Melt the butter in the microwave and add third.
  - Add the salt fourth.
  - Add the flour fifth.
  - Sprinkle the yeast on top.
- **Step 2**
  - Set the bread machine for a basic white loaf according to the machine instructions.

</Instructions>
