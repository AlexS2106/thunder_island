<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 500 g plain white flour
- 300 ml warm water
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp veg oil
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 ½ tsp active yeast

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Add the warm water to the bread machine first.
  - Add the honey and veg oil second.
  - Melt the butter in the microwave and add third.
  - Add the salt fourth.
  - Add the flour fifth.
  - Sprinkle the yeast on top.
- **Step 2**
  - Set the bread machine on the leavened dough cycle. (Number 8 on the Moulinex Pain Plaisir.)
- **Step 3**
  - When the cycle is finished, remove the dough onto a lightly floured surface.
  - Divide the dough into 10 equal parts, and roll into bun shapes.
  - Place the buns on a baking paper on a baking tray, and leave to rise for 2 hours.
- **Step 4**
  - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 220°C.
  - Bake buns on the middle shelf for 15 mins.
- **Step 5**
  - Remove the buns from the oven and leave to cool.

</Instructions>
