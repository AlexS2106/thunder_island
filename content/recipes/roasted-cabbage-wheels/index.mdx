<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 2 x large (2 x 250 g) cabbage slices
- 1 tbsp oil
- 2 x pinches of salt
- 2 x pinches of black pepper
- 2 x pinches of garlic powder

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 210Â°C.
- **Step 2**
  - Prepare a baking tray by brushing the base with oil.
  - Place the cabbage slices on top of the oil, with space around each slice.
  - Give the top of each cabbage slice a brush with the oil, take care to brush oil around the edges.
  - Sprinkle the pinches of salt, pepper and garlic powder over both cabbage slices.
- **Step 3**
  - Lightly cover the cabbage slice with a sheet of aluminium foil.
  - Roast in the oven for 45 mins.
- **Step 4**
  - Serve one cabbage wheel per person.

</Instructions>
