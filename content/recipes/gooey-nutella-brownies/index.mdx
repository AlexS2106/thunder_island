<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 280 g nutella
- 115 g butter
- 200 g sugar
- 2 x large eggs
- 95 g flour
- 25 g cocoa powder
- 1 tsp vanilla essence
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1 x small chocolate bar

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Grease some baking paper and use it to line a 23 cm wide baking tin. This is the medium sized pan for us.
- Roughly chop the chocolate bar.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Pre-heat a fan-assisted oven to 180°C.
- **Step 2**
  - Mix the dry ingredients in one bowl.
  - Soften the butter and mix the wet ingredients in another.
- **Step 3**
  - Combine and whisk till smooth.
  - Add the chocolate bar pieces and stir.
- **Step 4**
  - Pour the mixture into the baking tray on the baking paper.
- **Step 5**
  - Bake for 25 mins in the middle of the oven.
- **Step 6**
  - When the time is up, remove from the oven and leave for 30 mins.
- **Step 7**
  - Cut into squares and serve.

</Instructions>
