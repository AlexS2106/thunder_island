<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 2 x courgettes
- 1 x tomato
- 30 g apple
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2 x pinches dried basil

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Peel and decore the apple.
- Wash the courgette skins, then top and tail.
- Finely dice the tomato.
- Finely dice the apple

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 200Â°C.
- **Step 2**
  - Cut the courgettes in half, lengthways.
  - Scoop out the flesh in the centre; make sure to leave enough of the flesh at either end to keep it as a boat.
  - Keep the removed flesh.
- **Step 3**
  - Cover all sides of the courgettes with olive oil.
  - Place each boat on baking paper on a baking tray.
- **Step 4**
  - Mix the tomato, apple and courgette flesh.
  - Spoon the mix back into the courgette boats.
    -Sprinkle a pinch of basil onto each boat.
- **Step 5**
  - Bake in the oven for 20 mins.
- **Step 6**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
