### Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 185 g greek yoghurt
- 120 g mozzarella
- pinch of salt
- pinch of black pepper

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Preheat the oven to 200Â°C.
- **Step 2**
  - Line a baking tray with baking paper.
- **Step 3**
  - In a bowl, whisk the eggs.
  - Add the yoghurt and whisk.
  - Add the cheese and whisk.
- **Step 4**
  - Pour the mixture onto the baking paper and spread out into a thin layer.
- **Step 5**
  - Bake for 20 mins.
- **Step 6**
  - Cut to the size required and serve.

</Instructions>
