<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 130 g (1 medium) sweet potato
- 1 small can (110 g) undrained tin of tuna in oil
- ½ green bell pepper
- ½ medium onion
- 2 tsp smoked paprika
- ½ tsp thyme
- ¼ tsp garlic powder

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Peel and dice the sweet potato.
- Roughly dice the green bell pepper.
- Roughly dice the onion.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 200°C.
- **Step 2**
  - Mix all the ingredients in a 22cm x 22cm casserole dish, ensuring the oil from the tinned tuna covers all the vegetables.
- **Step 3**
  - Bake for 25 mins, stirring well halfway through.
- **Step 4**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
