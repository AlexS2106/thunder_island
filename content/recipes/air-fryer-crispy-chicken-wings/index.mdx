<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 1 kg chicken wings
  - 3 tsp baking powder
  - 1 tsp sweet paprika
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - 1 tsp salt
  - ½ tsp black pepper
  - ¼ tsp basil
  - ¼ tsp thyme
  - ¼ tsp ground ginger
  - ¼ tsp oregano
  - ¼ tsp mustard powder
  - sprays of oil

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Allow 1 hour for the chicken to marinate.
  - Remove the chicken from the fridge 15 minutes before cooking to warm up.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Add all the herbs and spices to a marinating bag.
  - **Step 2**
    - Pat dry the chicken wings with kitchen paper, then add the wings to the bag.
    - Seal the bag, and shake until all the chicken is covered.
    - Leave the chicken in the bag in the fridge for a minimum of 1 hour.
  - **Step 3**
    - Remove the chicken from the fridge and allow it to warm.
  - **Step 4**
    - Spritz the air fryer with oil and set it for 200 deg C and 30 mins.
  - **Step 5**
    - When ready, add the chicken wings to the air fryer and spritz the wings with oil.
  - **Step 6**
    - When the time is up, check the chicken is cooked and that the juices run clear before serving.

</Instructions>
