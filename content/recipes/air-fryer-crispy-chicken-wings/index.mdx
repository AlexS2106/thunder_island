### Ingredients

- 1 kg chicken wings
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 2 tsp salt
- sprays of oil

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Remove the chicken from the fridge 15 minutes before cooking to warm up.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Pat dry the chicken wings with kitchen paper, then add the wings to the bag.
  - Seal the bag, and shake until all the chicken is covered.
- **Step 2**
  - Spritz the air fryer with oil and set it for 200Â°C and 45 mins.
- **Step 3**
  - When ready, add the chicken wings to the air fryer and spritz the wings with oil.
- **Step 4**
  - When the time is up, check the chicken is cooked and that the juices run clear before serving.
- **Step 5**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
