<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 4 x chicken drumsticks
  - 1 tbsp lard
  - 1½ tsp salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Remove the chicken from the fridge 15 minutes before cooking to warm up.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 220°C.
    - Add the lard to a baking tray and place it into the oven as it heats.
  - **Step 2**
    - Pat dry the chicken drumsticks with kitchen paper.
    - Rub the salt into the chicken.
  - **Step 3**
    - When the oven is at full temperature, remove the baking tray with the now sizzling lard.
    - Place the chicken drumsticks into the hot lard, and turn to cover all sides with lard.
    - Return the baking tray and the drumsticks to the oven.
    - Roast for 40 minutes, turning the chicken part-way through.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
