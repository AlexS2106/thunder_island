### Ingredients

- 1 x broccoli head
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 tsp salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Remove the bottom of the broccoli stalk, where it's a little _woody_, and chop the rest of the broccoli, florets and stalk into bite-size florets.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 200Â°C.
- **Step 2**
  - Mix the broccoli with olive oil and salt.
- **Step 3**
  - Spread the broccoli out on baking paper on a baking tray.
  - Roast for 25 mins turning halfway through.
- **Step 4**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
