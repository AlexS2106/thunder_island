<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 1 x butternut squash
- 1 tbsp oil
- ½ tsp salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Peel and de-seed the butternut squash.
- Cut the squash into cubes about an 2cm square.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Pre-heat a fan-assisted oven to 200°C.
- **Step 2**
  - Mix the butternut squash cubes with oil and salt and spread the squash on a baking tray.
- **Step 3**
  - Roast for about 35 mins; turn the squash cubes about halfway through.
- **Step 4**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
