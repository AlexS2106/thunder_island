<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 250 g flour + extra for dusting
- 150 ml warm water
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp active yeast
- 4 tsp oil
- 1 x clove garlic
- ½ tsp dried rosemary

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- To keep the naan bread warm, preheat an oven to the lowest setting.
- Crush the garlic.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Mix the flour, salt and yeast in a bowl.
  - Add the water and mix in.
- **Step 2**
  - Leave in a warm place to rise for 1 hour.
- **Step 3**
  - Add the garlic and the rosemary and knead until everything is mixed.
  - Divide into 8 portions.
- **Step 4**
  - Roll out each portion into a circular shape of about ½cm thickness.
- **Step 6**
  - Heat a small frying pan on medium-high heat. (Number 7.)
  - Add ½ tsp of the sunflower oil to the pan and brush to cover the pan base.
- **Step 7**
  - Add one portion of the dough.
  - Cook for 1 min 30 secs on one side, flip, and cook for 1 min 30 secs on the other.
  - Remove from the pan and keep warm in the oven.
  - Repeat until all the bread is cooked.
- **Step 8**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
