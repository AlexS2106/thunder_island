<Ingredients>

### Ingredients🧂

- 1 kg chicken wings (separated into drumettes and flats)
- 150 g shredded parmesan cheese
- 6 eggs
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp smoked paprika

- Buffalo Sauce 🔥
  - 100 g butter
  - 1 tbsp hot sauce
  - Pinch of cayenne

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions 👨‍🍳

#### Preparation

- Seperate the wings into drumettes and flats.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Pat dry the chicken wings with kitchen paper.
- **Step 2**
  - Spritz the air fryer with oil and set it for 200°C and 45 mins.
- **Step 3**
  - Beat eggs in a bowl.
  - In a separate bowl, mix parmesan with optional spices.
  - Dip each wing in egg, then coat with parmesan mixture.
- **Step 4**
  - Melt the butter.
  - Mix the butter, hot sauce and cayenne.
  - Toss cooked wings in sauce until evenly coated.
- **Step 5**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
