### Ingredients

- 500 g beetroot
- 60 ml balsamic vinegar
- 400 ml water
- Â¼ tsp salt
- 2 tbsp ghee

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Top, tail and peel the beetroot.
- If any of the beetroots are very large, cut them in half.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Add the water and vinegar to a saucepan and give a quick stir.
  - Place the beetroot into the pan, the liquid should just cover the beetroots - if not, add a bit more water.
- **Step 2**
  - Set it to simmer for about an hour until the beetroot softens.
- **Step 3**
  - In a large frying pan, melt the ghee on a high heat. (Number 8.)
- **Step 4**
  - Drain the beetroot.
  - Place the beetroot on a chopping board, and press down on each one with a plate to 'smash' them.
- **Step 5**
  - Sprinkle the beetroot with salt.
- **Step 6**
  - Fry the beetroot for 1 minute on each side.
- **Step 7**
  - Serve by themselves or with a dip.

</Instructions>
