<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 100 g oats
  - 100 g self-raising flour 
  - 100 g butter
  - 100 g sugar
  - 1 tbsp  golden syrup
  - ½ tsp ground cinnamon

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 180°C.
  - **Step 2**
    - Place all the ingredients into a food processor and mix. It may be necessary to push some of the ingredients away from the sides of the processor in order to mix everything thoroughly.
  - **Step 3**
    - Take about 1 tbsp of the mixture and roll it into a ball in your hands, press both hands together to flatten the ball into a biscuit shape.
    - Place the biscuit onto a baking tray - allow plenty of space between each biscuit.
    - Repeat until you have approximately 12 biscuits.
  - **Step 4**
    - Bake in the middle of the oven for 15 mins, gently flip the biscuits at 10 mins.
  - **Step 5**
    - Remove from the oven and allow to cool.

</Instructions>
