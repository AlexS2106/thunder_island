<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 300 g cabbage
- 100 g fennel
- 3 x slices bacon
- 1 x clove garlic
- 1 tbsp lard
- ½ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp black pepper
- ¼ tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp smoked paprika

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Crush the garlic.
- Finely chop the fennel.
- Roughly slice the cabbage.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Melt the lard in a large frying pan on medium-high heat. (Number 7.)
- **Step 2**
  - Fry the bacon till crispy, remove and set aside.
- **Step 3**
  - Add the fennel to the pan and fry till soft, about 15 mins.
- **Step 4**
  - Add the cabbage, garlic, salt, black pepper, garlic powder and sweet paprika to the pan.
- **Step 5**
  - Turn the heat down to medium-low. (Number 3.)
  - Chop the bacon up small and return it to the frying pan.
  - Simmer on low heat to medium heat for 30 mins. Stir often and add more lard if the cabbage starts to stick.
- **Step 6**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
