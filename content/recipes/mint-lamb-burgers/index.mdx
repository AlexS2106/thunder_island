<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 500 g lamb mince
- 1½ tbsp dried mint
- 1½ tbsp dried chives
- ¼ tsp dried ground ginger
- 1 x clove garlic
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp lard

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Crush the garlic.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Place all ingredients in a bowl and mix the ingredients using your hands; thoroughly squash the ingredients together.
- **Step 2**
  - Melt the lard in a frying pan over medium-high heat. (Number 7.)
- **Step 3**
  - Fry for 4 mins on each side.
- **Step 4**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
