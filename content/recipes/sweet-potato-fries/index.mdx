<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 300 g (2 medium-sized) sweet potatoes 
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - ½ tsp salt
  - ¼ tsp black pepper

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Peel the sweet potato and chop it into fries.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 200°C.
  - **Step 2**
    - Mix the sweet potato fries, olive oil, salt and pepper.
    - Spread the sweet potato fries out on baking paper on a baking tray.
  - **Step 3**
    - Bake for 25 mins turning halfway through.
  - **Step 4**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
