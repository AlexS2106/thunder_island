<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 65 g self-raising flour
- 115 g butter
- 60g unsweetened cocoa powder
- 100 g sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla essence
- 2 eggs
- ¼ tsp salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Grease some baking paper and use it to line a 23 cm wide baking tin.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 160°C.
- **Step 2**
  - Melt the butter in the microwave.
- **Step 3**
  - Place all the ingredients, including the melted butter, into a food processor.
  - Pulse until the mixture it smooth.
- **Step 4**
  - Pour the mixture into the baking tray on the baking paper.
- **Step 5**
  - Bake for 35 mins in the middle of the oven.
- **Step 6**
  - When the time is up, remove the sponge from the oven and leave to cool.
  - When cool, remove from the tray by picking up the sides of the baking paper, set down and then peel back.
  - Cut the sponge into squares.

</Instructions>
