<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 500 g (2 large) chicken breast
  - 1 litre chicken stock
  - 120 ml coconut cream
  - ½ tbsp olive oil
  - ½ tsp turmeric
  - ½ tsp ground ginger
  - ¼ tsp garlic powder
  - ¼ tsp cinnamon
  - pinch black pepper

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Dice the chicken breasts.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Add the chicken breast, olive oil, chicken stock, coconut cream, turmeric, ground ginger, garlic powder, cinnamon, and black pepper into a large saucepan.
    - Bring the saucepan to a boil.
    - Simmer for 25 mins.
  - **Step 2**
    - Remove soup from the heat.
    - Use a handheld blender and partially shred the chicken meat.
  - **Step 3**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
