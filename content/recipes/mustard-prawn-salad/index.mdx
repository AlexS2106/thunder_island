<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 400 g de-shelled prawns
- 2 x tomatoes
- 2 x avocados
- 6 tbsp light olive oil
- 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 2 tsp Dijon mustard
- 2 x cloves garlic
- ¼ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp black pepper

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Crush the garlic
- Peel, de-stone and dice the avocado.
- Dice the tomatoes.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - In a small bowl, mix the olive oil, apple cider vinegar, Dijon mustard, garlic, salt and pepper to make the vinaigrette.
- **Step 2**
  - Mix the tomatoes, avocados, prawns and vinaigrette in a salad bowl.
- **Step 3**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
