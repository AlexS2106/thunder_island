<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 1 x avocado
- 3 tbsp tinned tuna
- 2 tsp mayonnaise

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Drain the tuna.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Cut the avocado in half and remove the stone.
- **Step 2**
  - In a small bowl, mix the tuna and mayonnaise.
  - Spoon the tuna mayonnaise into the avocado.
- **Step 3**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
