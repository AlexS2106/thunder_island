<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 400 g pork tenderloin
  - 1 tsp lard
  - ½ tsp salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Remove the pork from the fridge about 30 minutes before cooking to warm up.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 200°C.
    - Place a baking tray in the oven with the lard.
  - **Step 2**
    - Rub the pork all over with the salt.
  - **Step 3**
    - In the baking tray with the now hot lard, place the pork tenderloin and roll it on all sides to cover it with the ghee.
  - **Step 4**
    - Roast for 30 mins.
  - **Step 5**
    - Remove and wrap in aluminium foil for a further 20 mins.
  - **Step 6**
    - Cut and serve.

</Instructions>
