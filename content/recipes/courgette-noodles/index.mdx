<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 1 x courgette per person

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Top, tail and peel the courgette.
- Spiralise the courgette.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Place the spiralised courgette on a plate.
  - Microwave on high for 2-4 mins until heated through, 'fluff' the courgette partway through.
- **Step 2**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
