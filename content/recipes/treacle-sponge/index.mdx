<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 100 g self-raising flour
- 100 g butter
- 100 g sugar
- 2 x eggs
- 1 tsp baking powder
- ½ tsp vanilla essence
- 8 tbsp golden syrup
- 1 tsp butter

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Preheat the oven to 180°C.
- **Step 2**
  - In a 23cm x 23cm oven-proof dish, rub the bottom and all sides with the 1 tsp of butter.
  - Pour the golden syrup into the dish.
- **Step 3**
  - Mix the flour, butter, sugar, baking powder and vanilla essence until smooth.
  - Pour the sponge mixture on top of the syrup.
- **Step 4**
  - Bake in the oven for 20 mins.
- **Step 5**
  - Serve warm with cream or custard.

</Instructions>
