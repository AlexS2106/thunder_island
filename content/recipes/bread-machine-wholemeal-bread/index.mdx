<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 335 g wholemeal flour
- 200 ml warm water
- 2 tsp honey
- 2 tsp veg oil
- 2 tsp butter
- 2/3 tsp salt
- 1 tsp active yeast

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Add the warm water to the bread machine first.
  - Add the honey and veg oil second.
  - Melt the butter in the microwave and add third.
  - Add the salt fourth.
  - Add the flour fifth.
  - Sprinkle the yeast on top.
- **Step 2**
  - Set the bread machine on the wholemeal bread cycle. (Number 6 cycle and a 500 g loaf with a medium crust on the Moulinex Pain Plaisir.)

</Instructions>
