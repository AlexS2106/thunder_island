### Ingredients

- 250 ml cream
- 250 ml milk
- 85 g honey
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tsp vanilla essence
- Â¼ tsp salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Add the cream and milk to a pan and heat until hot but not boiling.
  - Remove from the heat and let cool to warm.
- **Step 2**
  - Whisk the egg yolks until they thicken and become pale.
- **Step 3**
  - When the cream and milk mixture has cooled, add the egg yolks while whisking.
  - Add the remaining ingredients and whisk.
- **Step 4**
  - Return the pan to the hob and bring to an almost boil, stirring gently.
  - Heat for about 15 mins until mixture thickens.
- **Step 5**
  - Remove from the heat and leave to cool.
- **Step 6**
  - Leave to cool, place in the fridge to chill for 2 hours then for the last 20 minutes transfer to the freezer.
- **Step 7**
  - Make according to the ice cream machine instructions.

</Instructions>
