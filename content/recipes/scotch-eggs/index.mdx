<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 500 g pork mince
- 1 tbsp lard
- ½ small onion
- 2 x cloves garlic
- 1 tbsp worcestershire sauce
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- ½ tsp black pepper
- ½ tsp smoked paprika
- ½ tsp dried sage
- 4 eggs
- 1 egg
- breadcrumbs
- flour
- oil

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Melt the lard.
- Finely chop the onion.
- Crush the garlic.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Soft boil four eggs.
- **Step 2**
  - Mix the pork mince, melted lard, onion, garlic, salt, pepper, paprika, sage, mustard and worcestershire sauce.
- **Step 3**
  - Divide the meat mixture into 4.
- **Step 4**
  - Make a flat disc of a meat portion, fold around a boiled egg and seal. Use water if necessary.
  - Repeat for three more times.
- **Step 5**
  - Wrap in cling film and place in the fridge for 30 mins.
- **Step 6**
  - Three bowls. One of beaten egg, one of flour, one of breadcrumbs.
- **Step 7**
  - Remove the eggs from the fridge, remove the wrap.
  - Dip flour, egg, breadcrumbs, egg, breadcrumbs.
- **Step 8**
  - Pre-heat a fan-assisted oven to 180°C.
  - Heat a pan of oil on a medium high heat. (Number 7.)
  - Fry each scotch egg. Make each brown on all sides.
- **Step 9**
  - Bake in the middle of the oven for 10 mins.
- **Step 10**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
