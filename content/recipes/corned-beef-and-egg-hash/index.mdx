<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 340 g (1 can) corned beef
- 5 x eggs
- ½ tbsp lard
- ¼ tsp salt
- ½ tsp mustard powder

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Dice the corned beef

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Heat the lard in a large frying pan on medium heat. (Number 6.)
- **Step 2**
  - When the lard is heated, add the corned beef, salt and mustard powder.
  - Fry for 10 mins, stirring often.
- **Step 3**
  - Crack the eggs in with the beef.
  - Keep stirring until the eggs have gone solid, about 3-4 mins.
- **Step 4**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
