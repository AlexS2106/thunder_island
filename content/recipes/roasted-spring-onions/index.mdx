<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 15-20 x spring onions
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- ¼ tsp salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Top, tail and peel off the outer layer of the spring onions.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 180°C.
- **Step 2**
  - Mix the spring onions, olive oil and salt together and spread them out on a baking tray.
- **Step 3**
  - Roasted for 25 mins; turn halfway through cooking.
- **Step 4**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
