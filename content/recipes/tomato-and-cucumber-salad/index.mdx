- 5 x tomatoes
- 1 x cucumber

- 60 ml coconut cream
- 1 tbsp water
- 2 tsp lemon juice
- pinch (large) salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Finely dice the tomatoes.
- Finely dice the cucumber.
- Finely slice the onion.
- Chop the mint leaves very finely.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Mix the mint leaves, coconut cream, lemon juice, and salt together in a small bowl.
- **Step 2**
  - Mix the tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion in a salad bowl.
- **Step 3**
  - Pour the mint dressing over the salad and mix well.
- **Step 4**
  - Serve at room temperature.

</Instructions>
