<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 2 x (large) parsnips
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1½ tsp salt
- 1 tsp curry powder

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Peel, top and tail, and slice the parsnips into 2cm cubes.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Pre-heat a fan-assisted oven to 200°C.
- **Step 2**
  - Mix the oil, salt and curry powder in a bowl and add to the parsnips; toss until each slice is thoroughly covered.
- **Step 3**
  - Bake for 20 mins, turning halfway through cooking.
- **Step 4**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
