<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 230 g tinned tuna
- 120 g onion
- 1 x clove garlic
- 2 tbsp tomato puree
- 1 x egg
- pinch salt
- pinch black pepper

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Finely chop the onion.
- Drain as much liquid as you can from the tinned tuna.
- Crush the garlic clove.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 180Â°C.
- **Step 2**
  - Mix the tuna, onion, garlic, tomato puree, egg, salt and black pepper in a bowl.
- **Step 3**
  - Cover the base of a baking tray with a baking sheet.
  - Take small amounts of the mixture.
  - Squeeze out any excess moisture.
  - Shape them into small patties about 5-7 cm in diameter.
  - Place each burger carefully on the paper.
- **Step 4**
  - Bake in the oven for 20 mins.
- **Step 5**
  - Serve hot or cold.

</Instructions>
