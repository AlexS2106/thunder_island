<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 1 x avocado
- 50 g cherry tomatoes
- 200 g smoked salmon
- 100 g cream cheese
- 4 x eggs
- 2 tsp butter
- pinch (large) salt

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Peel, de-seed and slice the avocado.
- Halve the tomatoes.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - To make the scrambled eggs, melt the butter in a medium frying pan over medium heat. (Number 5.)
  - Crack the eggs in a bowl, add the salt, and whisk with a fork.
  - Add the eggs to the frying pan, and stir often.
  - When the eggs are mostly but not entirely solid, remove them from the heat and divide them equally between two plates.
- **Step 2**
  - Divide all the remaining ingredients onto the two plates.
- **Step 3**
  - Wrap a slice of avocado and some cream cheese inside each smoked salmon slice.
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
