<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

  - **For the shrimp**
    - 400 g peeled shrimp
    - ½ tbsp lard
    - ¼ tsp salt
    - ⅛ tsp black pepper
  - **For the salad**
    - 135 g (¼ head) iceberg lettuce
    - 1 x avocado
    - 200 g (½ medium) cucumber 
    - 1 medium tomato
  - **For the dressing**
    - 2 tbsp olive oil
    - 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
    - ½ tbsp honey
    - pinch mustard powder

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Roughly chop the lettuce.
  - Chop the avocado.
  - Chop the cucumber.
  - Dice the tomato.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Melt the lard in a medium frying pan on medium heat. (I use the number 6.)
    - Season the shrimp with salt and pepper.
    - Fry the shrimp for about 5 mins turning halfway through; they will turn pink when done.
  - **Step 2**
    - Mix the lettuce, avocado and tomato.
  - **Step 3**
    - Whisk the olive oil, apple cider vinegar, honey and mustard in a small bowl.
  - **Step 4**
    - To serve, pour the dressing over the salad ingredients and place the shrimp on top.

</Instructions>
