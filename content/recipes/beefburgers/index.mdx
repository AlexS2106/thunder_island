<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- **For the burger**
  - 500 g beef mince
  - 1 x egg
  - ½ tsp salt
  - ½ tsp black pepper
  - ½ tbsp oil (for use in a frying pan)

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Take the meat out of the fridge 30 mins before use to warm up, Carl's fingers will thank you...

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - In a large bowl, mix the beef mince, egg, salt, and pepper.
- **Step 2**
  - Squish the burger mixture through your fingers until the texture becomes smooth and soft.
  - Divide the mixture into 4.
- **Step 3 _For the frying pan_**
  - Heat the oil in a large frying pan on a medium heat. (Number 6.)
- **Step 3 _For the George Forman Grill_**
  - Turn it on.
- **Step 4*For the frying pan***
  - Take a handful of the meat and drop it in the pan and use a spatula to pat it into a rough shape that will fit in a bun.
  - Fry the burgers on one side for 4 mins.
  - Carefully turn them over, press down a little to flatten, and fry on the other side for 3 mins.
  - Check to see if they are done, if not return to the heat and fry for longer.
- **Step 4*For the George Forman Grill***
  - Take a handful of the meat and splat it on the grill.
  - Lower the lid.
  - Wait 4 mins.
  - Remove the burger and splat the next one.
- **Step 5**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
