<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 3 x eggs
- 3 tbsp cream cheese
- ½ tsp baking powder

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 180°C.
- **Step 2**
  - Prepare a baking tray by covering it with baking paper.
- **Step 3**
  - Separate the eggs into two bowls, whites and yolks.
- **Step 4**
  - Whisk the egg whites until they make stiff peaks.
- **Step 5**
  - Whisk the egg yolks.
  - Add in the cream cheese and baking powder.
  - Whisk again.
- **Step 6**
  - Use the soup ladle and make 4 pools of the mixture onto the baking paper.
- **Step 6**
  - Bake in the centre of the oven for 15 mins, keep a close eye on the cooking.
- **Step 7**
  - After cooking, remove from the oven and leave to cool before peeling off the baking paper to use.

</Instructions>
