### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Peel the potatoes and chop into fries.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Zap the potatoes in the microwave for 10-15 mins, move them around occasionally to make sure the centre ones are also done.
- **Step 2**
  - Brush the chips with oil.
- **Step 3**
  - Air fry at 200Â°C for 12 mins, shake often.
- **Step 4**
  - Serve.
