<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 8 x asparagus spears
- 4 x bacon rashers
- 40 g cheddar cheese
- 1 tsp olive oil

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 200Â° C.
- **Step 2**
  - Wrap one rasher of bacon around two asparagus spears. Try to wind the bacon from top to bottom and cover most of the spears.
  - Place the bacon-wrapped asparagus in a casserole dish.
  - Pour over the olive oil and use a pastry brush to brush each bundle with oil.
- **Step 3**
  - Roast in the oven for 15 minutes.
- **Step 4**
  - Meanwhile, grate the cheese. Then, when the 15 minutes is up, sprinkle the cheese over the bacon asparagus bundles.
- **Step 5**
  - Set the oven timer for further 15 minutes.
- **Step 6**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
