<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 250 g flour + extra for dusting
- 120 ml warm water
- 1 tbsp lard
- ½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp baking powder

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Mix all the ingredients together.
- **Step 2**
  - Leave in a warm place for 1 hour.
- **Step 3**
  - Take 1 tbsp of the dough and roll it out as thinly as possible.
  - Repeat until all the dough is rolled out.
- **Step 4**
  - Heat a medium frying pan over medium-high heat. (Number 7.)
- **Step 6**
  - Cook each tortilla for 1 min on each side, and repeat until all the tortillas are cooked.
- **Step 7**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
