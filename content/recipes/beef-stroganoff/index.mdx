<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 400 g sirloin steak
- 1 x onion
- 150 g mushrooms
- 2 x cloves garlic
- 300 ml coconut cream
- 2 tbsp lard
- 1 x beef stock cube
- ½ tbsp white vinegar
- ½ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp black pepper
- ⅛ tsp mustard powder

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Cut the sirloin steak into strips.
- Finely chop the fennel.
- Slice the mushrooms.
- Crush the garlic.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Melt the lard in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. (Number 8.)
- **Step 2**
  - Cook the strips for 1 minute on each side or until just cooked.
  - Remove from the pan and set aside.
- **Step 3**
  - Turn the heat down to medium.
  - Fry the fennel until soft and slightly browned.
  - Add the garlic and mushrooms.
- **Step 4**
  - Turn the heat down to low.
  - Add the coconut cream, beef stock cube, white vinegar, salt, black pepper, and dry mustard.
  - Stir in.
- **Step 5**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
