<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 2 x mahi mahi fillets
- 1 tbsp lard
- 1 tsp dried parsley
- ¼ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp black pepper
- ¼ tsp garlic powder
- ¼ tsp cayenne pepper

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Remove the mahi mahi from the fridge about 30 mins before cooking to warm up.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Dry the mahi mahi by patting it with kitchen paper.
  - Mix all the above spices in a bowl and rub on both sides of each mahi mahi.
- **Step 2**
  - Heat the lard in a medium frying pan on medium-high heat. (Number 7.)
- **Step 3**
  - Place the spiced mahi mahi skin side down and cook for 4 mins.
  - Flip over and cook for another 4 mins.
  - When the mahi mahi is cooked, remove it from the heat.
  - A meat thermometer will read 52°C.
- **Step 4**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
