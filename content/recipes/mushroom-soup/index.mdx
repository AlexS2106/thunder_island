<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 75 g mushrooms
- 110g (1 x medium) onion
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 250 ml coconut cream
- 240 ml beef stock

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Finely chop the fennel.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Heat the olive oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat. (Number 6.)
- **Step 2**
  - Fry the fennel until it starts to brown; stir often.
- **Step 3**
  - Add the mushrooms and fry until they are soft.
- **Step 4**
  - Add the beef stock cube and the coconut cream and stir in.
- **Step 5**
  - Turn the heat down to low until the sauce is barely simmering.
  - Leave to simmer for 20 mins.
- **Step 6**
  - Divide the mushroom sauce into two halves.
  - Puree half of the sauce with a food processor or handheld blender.
  - Stir the two halves back together again.
- **Step 7**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
