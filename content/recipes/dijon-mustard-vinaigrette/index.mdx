<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 6 tbsp light olive oil
  - 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  - 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  - 2 x cloves garlic
  - ¼ tsp salt
  - ¼ tsp black pepper

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Crush the garlic cloves

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Whisk all the ingredients together.
  - **Step 2**
    - Pour over a salad.

</Instructions>
