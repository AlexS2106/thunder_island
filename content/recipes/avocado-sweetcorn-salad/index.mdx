<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- **For the salad**
  - 300 g avocado
  - 125 g sweetcorn
- **For the dressing**
  - 3 tbsp lime juice
  - 1 tsp dried basil
  - Â½ tsp salt
  - pinch black pepper

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Dice the avocado.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - In a medium size bowl, mix the avocado and sweetcorn.
- **Step 2**
  - In a small bowl, mix the lime juice, basil, salt and pepper.
- **Step 3**
  - Pour the dressing over the avocado and sweetcorn and serve.

</Instructions>
