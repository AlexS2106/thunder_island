### Ingredients

  - 1 tsp lard/sunflower oil

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Place the cauliflower in a food processor and pulse until it resembles fine grains.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Melt the lard in a medium frying pan over medium heat. (I use the number 7.)
  - **Step 2**
    - When the lard is hot, pour in the cauliflower rice.
    - Fry the cauliflower for 10 mins stirring often.
  - **Step 3**
    - Serve.

</Instructions>
