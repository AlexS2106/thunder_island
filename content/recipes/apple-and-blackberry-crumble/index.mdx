<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- **For the filling**
  - 350 g apple
  - 150 g blackberries
  - 50 g caster sugar
- **For the crumble**
  - 300 g plain flour
  - 150 g cold butter
  - 75 g demarara sugar

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Peel, core and dice the apple.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 180Â°C.
- **Step 2**
  - Mix the filling ingredients in a 23cm x 23cm oven dish.
- **Step 3**
  - In a bowl, add the crumble ingredients together.
- **Step 4**
  - Rub the ingredients between your hands until the butter is combined. The ingredients will feel crumbly.
- **Step 5**
  - Bake in the oven for 30 mins.
- **Step 6**
  - Serve hot or cold with cream or custard.

</Instructions>
