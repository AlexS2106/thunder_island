<Ingredients>

### Ingredients
    
  - 200 g pasta
  - 50 g mozzarella cheese
  - ½ tbsp butter
  - 50 g onion
  - 1 x clove garlic
  - 1 tbsp plain flour
  - 150 ml milk
  - 400 g baked beans
  - 25 g chorizo
  - **For the topping**
    - 50g mozzarella cheese

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

  - Grate the mozzarella.
  - Dice the onion.
  - Crush the garlic.
  - Dice the chorizo.

#### Method

  - **Step 1**
    - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 175°C.
  - **Step 2**
    - Set the pasta to boil.
  - **Step 3**
    - Melt the butter in a small frying pan over medium heat.
    - Fry the onion until soft.
    - Add the garlic and chorizo and fry for 1 minute.
  - **Step 4**
    - Turn the heat down to medium-low.
    - Add the milk and 50g of mozzarella.
    - Stir until the mozzarella is melted.
  - **Step 5**
    - Add the flour and stir in.
    - Remove from the heat.
  - **Step 6**
    - Grease a large casserole dish.
    - Drain the cooked pasta and add it to the dish.
    - Stir in the milk-cheese mixture.
  - **Step 7**
    - Sprinkle the remaining 50g of mozzarella over the pasta.
    - Bake for 20 minutes.

</Instructions>
