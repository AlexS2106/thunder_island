<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 200 g mixed bell peppers
- 50 g goat's cheese
- 30 g shallots
- 6 x eggs
- 60 g cream
- 1 x garlic clove
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1 tbsp butter

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- De-seed and thinly slice the bell peppers.
- Peel and finely slice the shallots.
- Crush the garlic.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Pre-heat a fan-assisted oven to 200° C.
- **Step 2**
  - Mix the eggs, bell peppers, goat's cheese, shallots, cream and salt in a bowl.
- **Step 3**
  - In a 23cm x 23cm casserole dish, use a pastry brush to brush the butter over the bottom and sides of the dish.
  - Pour the egg mixture into the casserole dish.
  - Bake for 30 mins.
- **Step 4**
  - Work a spatula around all sides of the dish to loosen the frittata before removing it from the dish.
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
