<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 4 x chicken thighs (boneless and skinless)
- 2 tbsp lard
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ tsp black pepper

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Remove the chicken from the fridge 30 mins before cooking.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Melt the lard in a frying pan on high heat. (Number 8.)
- **Step 2**
  - Dry the chicken thighs with a kitchen towel and rub them with salt and pepper.
- **Step 3**
  - Add the chicken thighs to the pan and cook for 10-15 mins turning partway through. The chicken is cooked when a meat thermometer reads 75°C.
- **Step 4**
  - Serve.

</Instructions>
