<Ingredients>

### Ingredients

- 180 g (1 medium) apple
- 130 g (1 medium) sweet potato
- 70 g (1 medium) parsnip
- 2 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp oil
- ½ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp black pepper
- ⅛ tsp ground cinnamon

</Ingredients>

<Instructions>

### Instructions

#### Preparation

- Peel, de-core and roughly chop the apple.
- Peel and dice the sweet potato.
- Peel, top and tail and dice the parsnip.

#### Method

- **Step 1**
  - Preheat a fan-assisted oven to 200°C.
- **Step 2**
  - Mix the oil, salt, black pepper, ground cinnamon, and all the vegetables.
- **Step 3**
  - Line a baking tray with baking paper.
  - Spread the vegetables out evenly on the tray.
  - Drizzle the honey over the vegetables.
- **Step 4**
  - Roast in the oven for 30 mins; turn the vegetables halfway through the cooking time.
- **Step 5**
  - Serve half for a main meal or a quarter as a side dish.

</Instructions>
